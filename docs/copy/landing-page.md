## 2 · The why
*Make the stakes personal and human — about the kids. Hopeful, not heavy.*

- **Headline:** A whole generation is hurting where we can't see it.
- **Body:** Right now, a teenager someone loves is sitting in a classroom carrying more than they
  know how to say. Anxiety. Depression. The quiet fear that if they spoke up, no one would really
  understand. So they go quiet — and the people who love them never see it coming.
  We're making this film because that silence is costing us our kids. And because no one was ever
  meant to carry it alone.

- **Headline:** A film about being seen.
- **Body:** *The Silence Between Us* is a powerful and necessary film about the anxiety and depression
  so many teenagers face within the school system — and the fear and uncertainty that often keep them

  and on the simple, life-changing importance of being **seen, heard, and understood.**
