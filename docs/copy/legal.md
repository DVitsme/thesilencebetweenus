## /legal/contributions — Contribution & Refund Policy
*The most important and most custom page — it defines what a "supporter contribution" is.*

**What your contribution is.** When you support *The Silence Between Us*, you're making a voluntary
**contribution toward the production of an independent film**, in exchange for the supporter perks
described at checkout (e.g., a credit, set updates, a virtual meet & greet). It's a form of
**rewards-based crowdfunding**.

**What it is not.**
- It is **not a tax-deductible charitable donation.** *The Silence Between Us* is produced by KC
  Films & Media, a for-profit company, so contributions are not tax-deductible. We do not provide
  charitable tax receipts.
- It is **not an investment.** You receive no ownership, equity, profit share, or financial return of
  any kind.

**Perks & fulfillment.** We'll make reasonable, good-faith efforts to deliver the perks for your
tier. Because filmmaking timelines shift, exact dates may change, and the form of a perk may be
adjusted if necessary — if that happens, we'll offer a comparable alternative. Perks that require
your input (like how your name appears in the credits) depend on your timely response.

**Refunds.** Contributions go directly into production and are generally **non-refundable**. That
said, we're reasonable people: if you were charged in error, made a duplicate contribution, or have a
genuine concern, contact us at **kevin@kcfilmsmedia.com** within **[14] days** and we'll work with
you in good faith.

**Payments.** Payments are processed securely by **Stripe**. We never receive or store your full card
details. Charges appear on your statement as **[STATEMENT DESCRIPTOR]**.

**Questions?** Email **kevin@kcfilmsmedia.com**.
